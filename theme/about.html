This is the about page on the dogs theme. I have always found dogs and their interaction with humans interesting. And when I saw the diversity of breeds that exist, it got me even more curious. This theme only takes a look on 7 dog breeds.

The breed depicted in the home and about page is not listed among the 7, but was chosen for how it gives a sense of being part of multiple breeds. This breed however, is called the Welsh Springer Spaniel.
