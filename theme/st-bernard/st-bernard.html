St. Bernard

As adult

Cassie J [CC BY 2.0], via Wikimedia Commons

The St. Bernard, although being a massive dog, is usually depicted as a loyal and helpful dog. Usually seen in cartoons as first aid animals, coming to the rescue of mountain hikers. It is interesting to note that this type of dog was bred for rescue in the alps between Italy and Switzerland.

As puppy

dbking [CC BY 2.0], via Wikimedia Commons

The St. Bernard is usually seen having a calm disposure and a white fur covered with large brown spots. This dog was named after an italian monk named Bernard of Menthon.
