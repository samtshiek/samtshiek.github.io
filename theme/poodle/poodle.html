Poodle

As adult

H.Heuer [Public domain], via Wikimedia Commons

Contrary to what some could think at first, Poodles are not born with such elaborate hairstyles as we often see on television, but are cut to the owner's content and imagination as they are gifted with so much hair. Not only are they gifted with a lot of hair, but also with a lot of intelligence as they are ranked to be the second smartest breed of dogs.

As puppy

V. Eickenberg [Public domain], via Wikimedia Commons

Poodles come in three categories Standard Poddle, Miniature Poodle and Toy Poodle.

Poodles have a tendency to be very protective of their homes, and are very playful, carrying lots of energy especially as puppies still growing up.
