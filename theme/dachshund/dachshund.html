Dachshund

As adult

Pleple2000 [CC BY-SA 3.0], via Wikimedia Commons

The Dachshund is the kind of dog we usually see on television wearing some kind of hotdog outfit as comedy. This makes some sense because of its shape, as it is also called by some "Wiener dog". Its name also suggests the purpose people had for it as it is German for badger dog.

As puppy

harperlauren [CC BY-SA 2.0], via Wikimedia Commons

The Dachshund has incredible scent and was also used to hunt down small animals as rabbits. It is quite surprising given its small legs and long body.
