The Havanese is what I call the superstar in the dog world, since most of the time I have seen them depicted in shows or movies, they are usually in the company of a superstar. This goes without say when one considers the natural fabulous hair flowing down this breed's body.

As puppy

Lucky11 [CC BY-SA 3.0], via Wikimedia Commons

It is important to note that this breed's name Havanese comes from Havana, as it is called a cuban dog. It is actually called the National dog of cuba, isn't that interesting? I wonder what is considered the national dog in other countries.
