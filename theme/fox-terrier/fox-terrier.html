Fox Terrier

As adult

State Farm [CC BY 2.0], via Wikimedia Commons

The Fox-Terrier are a breed that is usually depicted in movies as a courageous companion, ready to defy danger despite its size. The Fox terrier are divided into 2 groups which are smooth and wired according to their fur.

As puppy

Gabriel Potvin [CC BY-SA 3.0], via Wikimedia Commons

Fox Terrier were bred in England during the 18th century, and as the name suggests, they were used to lure foxes out of their homes for hunting.
